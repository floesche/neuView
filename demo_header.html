L M B R Header Navigation Demo

This demonstrates the new compact navigation design where L (Left), M (Middle), B (Both), and R (Right) letters appear on the right side of the neuron type header, replacing the previous button-style navigation below the header.

Key Features:

  • Compact Design: Letters (L M B R) are positioned on the right side of the h1 title
  • Current Page Indicator: The current page letter is larger and highlighted with a background
  • Interactive Links: Other available pages are clickable links with hover effects
  • Responsive: On smaller screens, the letters move below the title for better mobile experience
  • Tooltip Support: Each letter has a descriptive tooltip explaining what it links to

Responsive Behavior

Try resizing your browser window to see how the navigation adapts:
